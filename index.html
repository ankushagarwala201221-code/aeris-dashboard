Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is VRI?

VRI estimates atmospheric visibility using pollution, humidity, rainfall and ambient light conditions.

❓ Why is PM2.5 dangerous?

PM2.5 particles reduce atmospheric clarity and affect respiratory health.

❓ How does humidity affect visibility?

Humidity creates microscopic droplets which increase fog formation.

❓ What happens during low VRI?

Low VRI indicates dangerous environmental visibility conditions.
